What is this Weather App?

This Weather App provides real-time weather updates, forecasts, and other weather-related data based on your location or a searched city.

How does the Weather App get its data?

The app fetches weather data from reliable APIs such as OpenWeatherMap or WeatherAPI to ensure accurate and up-to-date information.

Is the Weather App free to use?

Yes, this Weather App is completely free to use.

Can I check the weather for any city?

Absolutely! You can search for the weather of any city worldwide by entering its name in the search bar.

Does the website provide pressure , humidity and wind speed?

Yes, the Weather App offers these all.

Can I use this Weather App on mobile devices?

Yes, the app is fully responsive and works smoothly on both mobile and desktop devices.
